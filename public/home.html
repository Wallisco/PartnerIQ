Questions

Frequently asked

Is this a psychological test? Is it HPCSA-regulated in South Africa?
No. PartnerIQ is a business decision-support tool, not a classified psychological instrument. The HPCSA in South Africa regulates formal psychometric tests administered in employment or clinical contexts by registered professionals. PartnerIQ is a self-administered team intelligence and partnership assessment tool — closer to a structured business framework than a clinical instrument. You do not need a registered psychometrist or psychologist to use it. That said, we always recommend treating any personality data with the seriousness it deserves and not using it as the sole basis for hiring or business decisions.
How is PartnerIQ different from DiSC, CliftonStrengths, or MBTI?
Three main differences. First, scientific foundation: PartnerIQ is built on the Big Five (OCEAN) model — the most academically validated personality framework in existence, with 54 meta-analyses covering 554,778 individuals. DiSC, CliftonStrengths, and MBTI use proprietary or less-validated frameworks. Second, team-native design: those tools are built for individuals and require a facilitator to derive group insights. PartnerIQ generates group compatibility, team dynamics, and hiring briefs automatically. Third, actionability: our reports don't stop at "here's your personality type." They tell you specifically who to hire next, what role, with what OCEAN profile — tied to your actual business context.
Does everyone in the group have to pay?
No. The assessment is completely free for every participant. Each person verifies their email, completes 20 questions, and receives their own personal AI-generated profile by email — for free. The group organiser pays once to unlock the full group report. That single payment covers the entire group regardless of size (up to the participant limit for your tier).
What happens after I pay? How do I access my report?
After payment, you receive your results code by email. This code is the key to your group's full report. Go to the app, click "View report," enter your results code, and the full report generates immediately using AI. If you lose your results code, use the "Recover it by email" link on the site to have it resent to the email you used when creating the group.
Can I use PartnerIQ for an existing team, not just a founding team?
Yes — and this is one of the most valuable use cases. When creating a group, set the team context fields (business stage, team level, vertical, leader accountability) and the AI generates a context-aware culture diagnosis, gap analysis, internal improvement plan, and role-specific hiring briefs. This works for Sales teams, Operations teams, Finance teams, C-suites, or any functional group at any stage of business.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and through Paystack for South African cardholders (including South African bank cards, Instant EFT, and mobile money). Both payment methods are secure, PCI-compliant, and process instantly. Your results code is sent immediately after payment clears.
Is my personality data private? Who sees it?
Each participant's OCEAN profile and AI-generated individual report are sent only to their own verified email address. The full group report — including all individual profiles — is accessible only to the group organiser via the private results code. Participant data is stored securely in an encrypted database and is not shared with third parties. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@partneriq.fit.
